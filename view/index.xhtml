Namecoins?

A decentralized p2p system, providing an alternative to the common tier based and government controlled DNS infrastructure. It is based on the bitcoin technology.
It enables you to register and manage .bit domains without worries about outside influence.

To learn more about it, visit dot-bit.org.

Pooled mining

Namecoins are mined by finding blocks, the finder receives 50 namecoins for their effort, while everybody else doesn't see any reward. Additionally, the difficulty of finding blocks increases as more people join the mining effort.

Pooled mining is a joined effort that combines the resources of many people to find a block together, and share the reward in a fair distribution.
