Get mining tools and start digging

To join this pool, all you need is a namecoin address where we can send you your reward. You use this address instead of a username for your miner, the password has to be "none" (without quotation marks).

A way to get a new address to use is with namecoind getaccountaddress "" More details can be found at the bitcoin wiki

The pool runs at pool.namebit.org:9098

Rates

Our Numero Uno priority is protecting your namecoins (NMC). For that reason, we monitor every payout manually with full paranoia. Payouts should appear within a few blocks of the found block.

The bonus for being the block winner is 5 NMC.

44 shares are awarded based on the number of shares your miner submitted to the pool.
We sum your non-stale, unrejected shares since the last time we found a block, and distribute proportionally among all participants.

To cover the ongoing costs for this service, the pool keeps 1 coin of the reward per generated block.

Miner setup

PyOpenCL bitcoin miner with a GUI frontend poclbm.py --user addr --pass none --host pool.namebit.org --port 9098

OpenCL/CUDA GPU miner rpcminer-opencl.exe -url=http://pool.namebit.org:9098 -user=addr -password=none

Phoenix Miner phoenix.exe -u http://addr:none@pool.namebit.org:9098/ -k poclbm VECTORS BFI_INT AGGRESSION=10 WORKSIZE=128 DEVICE=0 FASTLOOP=false

CPU Miner minerd --userpass addr:none --url http://pool.namebit.org:9098
