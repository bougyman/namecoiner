Get mining tools and start digging

To join this pool, all you need is a namecoin address where we can send you your reward. You use this address instead of a username for your miner, the password has to be "none" (without quotation marks).

A way to get a new address to use is with namecoind getaccountaddress "" More details can be found at the bitcoin wiki

The pool runs at pool.namebit.org:10000

Rates

Our Numero Uno priority is protecting your namecoins (NMC). For that reason, we monitor every payout manually with full paranoia. Payouts should appear within a few blocks of the found block.

49 shares are awarded based on the number of shares your miner submitted to the pool since the last block the pool won (a "round")
We sum your non-stale, unrejected shares since the last time we found a block, and distribute proportionally among all participants.

To cover the ongoing costs for this service, the pool keeps 1 coin of the reward per generated block.

Miner setup

Please see the documentation for each miner for more information on their flags, all instructions here are submitted by our users and won't neccesarily reflect the options best for your machine.

PyOpenCL bitcoin miner with a GUI frontend poclbm.py --user addr --pass none --host pool.namebit.org --port 10000

OpenCL/CUDA GPU miner rpcminer-opencl.exe -url=http://pool.namebit.org:10000 -user=addr -password=none

Phoenix Miner phoenix.exe -u http://addr:none@pool.namebit.org:10000/ -k poclbm VECTORS BFI_INT AGGRESSION=10 WORKSIZE=128 DEVICE=0 FASTLOOP=false

CPU Miner minerd --userpass addr:none --url http://pool.namebit.org:10000

Ufasoft Miner bitcoin-miner.exe -o http://pool.namebit.org:10000 -p none -u addr
